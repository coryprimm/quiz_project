Welcome Everyone!

Quiz yourself was created to accelerate the speed of learning. By allowing you to tag what terms you need more work on you can breeze past what you already know.

How to start

  • Step 1: Create a new study topic, by clicking "Create Quiz".
    Or enter a pre-existing topic by clicking on the button associated with it below.

  • Step 2: Enter terms to study and their answers by clicking add to quiz and submitting the appropriate info.

  • Step 3: Take a random quiz when you have all the terms you want to remember submitted by clicking "Random Quiz". And that's it. As you take the random quiz you can mark the questions as easy or hard and then focus your time on the ones that are hard!

  • Step 4: Ok I lied but it was for your own good. The final step is to retake your original quiz or the easy quiz once you are sure you have all the terms down. If you mess up, at least you know you studied optimally and you will get it.
